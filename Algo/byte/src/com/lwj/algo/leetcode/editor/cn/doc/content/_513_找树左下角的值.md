<pre>

<strong>输出: </strong>1
</pre>

<p><img src="https://assets.leetcode.com/uploads/2020/12/14/tree2.jpg" style="width: 242px; " /><strong> </strong></p>

<strong>输入: </strong>[1,2,3,4,null,5,6,null,null,7]
<strong>输出: </strong>7
</pre>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
